About me

H e is a full-stack web developr and has a strong skill set in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, Python, Django. He is also well familiar with Object Oriented Programming (OOP). He has a deep understanding of how to create responsive and user-friendly web pages that are both visually appealing and easy to use. He has designed and developed attractive and prefect web pages. He works as a freelance web developer and loves programming. He is also a certified trainer of American councils and conducts free English language courses for Afghan immigrants living in Tajikistan. .

About me 2

H e was a professional lawyer and worked as a Defense attorney with (AIBA) the Afghanistan Independent Bar Association for several years. Being a defense lawyer, he served as a Human rights activist for the implementation of laws, human rights, and women's rights. Throughout his professional life, he tried to be a supporter of those who were oppressed by powerful figures Additionally, he allotted his time to volunteering for CTEO humanitarian organization (a local nonprofit organization in Afghanistan) serving the internally displaced people by launching campaigns and collecting aid for flood-affected families and setting up educational, legal, and cultural seminars for university students .
